Why this pitch works

Built to turn searches into orders.

The goal is not just a nicer design. It is a practical restaurant site that helps customers pick food, call, get directions, and place pickup orders without hunting through social media or menu photos.

☑

Mobile-first menu

Customers can search, filter by category, and add items to cart from one clean page.

🛍

Online ordering

Pickup orders collect customer details, notes, quantities, and order totals.

📍

Local visibility

Clear phone, hours, address, directions, and structured content improve trust and usability.

Real photos

Restaurant images, not generic stock.

This version uses public Monte Alban food and menu photos so the pitch feels specific to the actual restaurant.

Order online

Simple pickup flow customers understand.

This prototype lets customers add menu items, review their cart, enter pickup details, and submit a pickup order. It can later connect to Square, Toast, Clover, Stripe, Resend, Twilio, or a custom Cloudflare backend.
